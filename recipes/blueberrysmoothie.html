Blueberry Smoothie Recipe

Description

This smoothie is quick, easy to make, and will have you enjoying breakfast in a way you never have. It's low on calories as well as effort, so there is never any excuse to not eat breakfast again. With just a few simple ingredients and a blender, you could have a healthy breakfast in minutes. You can even increase the recipe to make enough for two people or even make enough to meal prep! The shake will hold for a few days in the fridge, but it is best served soon after making it.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of frozen blueberries
  • 1/2 banana
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablesoon of cashew butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Optional Toppings

  • Almonds
  • Banana
  • shredded coconut

Steps

  1. Put all of the ingredients into a blender and mix until smooth.
  2. Top with your favorite toppings and serve cold
